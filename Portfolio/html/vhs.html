VHS self portrait rig made in collaboration with New York based video artist Benton C Bainbridge. Audio responsive projections are mapped onto the body of the subject as song of their choice is played. The subject is then filmed on a VHS camera that is run through an analog video mixer and a CRT television before being digitally recorded. This self portrait, was the first test comlpleted with the rig.
